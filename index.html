Interior Design services

Bespoke designs and project management to make your property a home.

Our Interior Designers have worked on some of the most prestigious properties in the world and deliver a unique service to our clients which encompasses a full managed service. Our Interior Design team primarily work on post sale properties across new builds, townhouses, country estates and holiday homes. The Fine & Country designers bring a diverse range of skills across design and project management, enabling them to manage large scale renovation projects, inclusive of interior architecture. We offer a bespoke, luxury service, where our designers bring their wealth of expertise to tailor the design to your specific style, needs and character to truly turn your property into a home. Their goal is to offer you a fully managed service where required, involving you as and when necessary in the design decisions to ensure your comfort throughout the process.

Staging and styling services

Tailored solutions to maximise the value of your home on the market.

Our network of Staging & Styling partners provide expert and professional advice, tailored to the needs of the local market, the target demographic and the individual home owner. This showcases a property at its best to minimise sales time and achieve a property’s true value in the market. Our experts have extensive experience from furnishing show homes to staging country estates - each approached with the view of maximising enquiries and ultimately the value of a property in the market. Our experts do this by providing tailored packages that combine aesthetic fixes, furniture and accessories to maximise the use of space, lighting and a property’s unique characteristics. We only work with partners who use real size furniture to accurately represent a property, ensuring your investment achieves a return by maximising the sales value beyond the pure generation of enquiries.
